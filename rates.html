Visualizing Female Cancer Rates in the UK (2007).

In Spring 2014, I had to visualize cancer rates for my information design course. I did it in Photoshop for the course, and then recreated it with d3.js below. Hover over circles for exact numbers.

Circles are sized relative to number of females in each cancer group per 1000 females in each age group.
